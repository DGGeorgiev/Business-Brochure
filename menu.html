Our Menu

Our menu is fully vegetarian, and includes vegan and gluten-free options. We proudly serve our favourite local roasters Jojo Coffee and Silk Road Tea from Tofino, with an offering of sweet and savoury pastries from various local businesses such as: The French Square, Petite quiches, amazing brownies by the Gluten-Free Epicureans, vegan cheesecake cups, and lots of pet-themed treats!
